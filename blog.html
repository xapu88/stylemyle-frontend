The One Animal Print We Need

July 27, 2019

Trending

There are multitude of animal prints this season but only one to rule them all: step back cheetah print, the snakeskin is here! While you can still wear your leopard midi skirt of course, python in all its variations is what you need to play things up a bit. Head-to-toe looks great during Fashion Week, but real life asks for something more toned down and equally fun, so go all out on the accessories. Bags, shoes, sandals in snakeskin are the new neutrals, so pair them up with white, cream, brown and black for minimalistic tone-on-tone feel or take a 180 degree turn and use them against bright colors for added contrast. You will win either way - it's time to find your new staples on Stylemyle.
